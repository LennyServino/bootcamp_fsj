Omelette Recipe

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs room temperature
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

  • Beat the egss and milk until light and forthy
  • Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter
  • Pour in the eggs. Let them set for few seconds
  • Use a spatula to push the cooked eggs from the edge of the skillet toward
  • When the eggs are mostly set but still soft, fold in half
  • Slide the omelette onto a plate
